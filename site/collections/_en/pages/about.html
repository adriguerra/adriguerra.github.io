Born and raised in Geneva, Switzerland, I grew up in a household shaped by diplomacy and diversity—both my Guatemalan parents worked at the UN.

Little did I know that their global mindset would fuel my adventurous career journey.

My life so far has been a mix of bold pivots, steep learning curves, and a whole lot of passion.

It all started in the high-stakes world of commodity trading. While I was grateful for the incredible opportunities, the finance grind didn’t spark joy in me.

So, I took a leap and launched a cannabis startup. The highs were high (no pun intended) and the lows were low. I learned firsthand that startup life was no walk in the park.

Realizing I needed to level up, I stepped into the consulting world. My first stop was Capgemini as a Data Analyst. The reality was more like playing data janitor for legacy systems. Not exactly thrilling.

Then came OWT, a place buzzing with entrepreneurial energy. It was there I learned to think big, take risks, and craft strategies that actually work—skills I still lean on every day.

When I ventured into freelancing as a data scientist, I hit another realization: technical skills alone weren’t enough to make it in this fast-paced, competitive world.

I had to master the art of selling myself, fast.

As I hustled, I realized I didn’t want to just survive—I wanted to thrive.

That’s when I found my true calling in marketing, a field where I could combine creativity, storytelling, and technical expertise.

From this passion, Adaminds was born.

Today, I help e-commerce businesses craft digital experiences that captivate, engage, and drive meaningful growth.

It’s been a wild ride so far!

And I wouldn’t have it any other way, but maybe that's just me.
