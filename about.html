About Me

Hey there, I’m Adrian!

Thanks for stopping by.

I’ve tried on many hats, but the one that fits me best says Marketing Scientist. This passion led me to found Adaminds, a marketing science agency where creativity meets tech.

What’s a Marketing Scientist?

Think of me as a curious explorer, uncovering insights from data and turning them into marketing magic. If Sherlock Holmes loved A/B testing, he'd be consulting at Adaminds.

Previous Adventures 🚀

I founded Cebedia, an e-commerce startup in the cannabis industry. We started as the Vivino of CBD, later pivoting to the B2B space with a cannabis trading platform. It wasn’t all smooth sailing, but the lessons learned were invaluable.

Corporate Chapters

  • Koch Supply and Trading: Data Science prodige before Data Science was even cool.
  • Capgemini: Data Analyst a.k.a. data janitor cleaning up legacy systems.
  • OWT: Business Analyst learning to think and strategize like a pro.

Why Marketing?

It’s the perfect mix of creativity, storytelling, and data. Every campaign feels like solving a puzzle, and I love putting the pieces together to help businesses grow.
